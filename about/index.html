Megus
Music Demoscene About

Megus is an electronic music producer and demoscener based in Richmond, BC, Canada. He started writing music on ZX Spectrum back in the 90s. Megus was a founding member of the Brainwave demoscene group.

In 2010, Seajeff and Megus released “Around Past”, a digital fusion album that was important in developing Megus’ musical style. While he’s not limiting himself to one genre, he often returns to his roots and brings elements of chiptune and demoscene music to his works.

The new concept album “Yet Another Day” is the evolution of the style and the tribute to musical influences.
