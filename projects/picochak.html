PICOCHAK

PICOCHAK is a demoscene production (or, simply, a demo) for PICO-8 fantasy console. It’s a funny little demo about fictional space folks, which look surprisingly similar to Tatar national food, and their fight with evil donuts. My friends and I created it for CAFe’2019 demoparty, where it took the 2nd place in the console demo compo.
